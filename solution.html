Indo-Pacific humpback dolphin needs people's help
中华白海豚需要人们的帮助

Conservation Status 保护情况

In Hong Kong, the population is protected by the Wild Animals Protection Ordinance, Marine Park Ordinance, and the Environmental Impact Assessment Ordinance. In mainland Chinese waters, the species is a Protected Species of the First Order. Bangladesh is also currently establishing a Marine Protected Area to protect these dolphins from further harm.

在香港，中华白海豚受到《野生动物保护条例》、《海岸公园条例》和《环境影响评估条例》的保护。在中国大陆水域，该物种是一级保护物种。孟加拉国目前也在建立海洋保护区，以保护这些海豚免受进一步伤害。

What Can Be Done To Protect Them 怎样保护它们

According to conservation scientists and local NGOs, here are the recommendations for immediate actions to protect Indo-Pacific humpback dolphins:

根据保育科学家及非政府组织的建议，以下是可以为保护中华白海豚而立即采取的行动：

1. Establish a ban on gill and trammel nets in their habitats.

禁止在中华白海豚栖息地使用刺网。

2. New coastal developments should be located away from their habitats.

新的沿海开发项目应远离其栖息地。

3. Establish mandatory routes and speed limits for vessels to reduce both noise and the risk of vessel strikes.

为船只制定必须履行的路线和速度限制，以降低噪音和减少船只撞击的风险。

4. Reduce the release of pollution (air, water, soil).

采取相应措施减少空气、水源和土壤污染的排放。

5. Increase natural river flows.

采取相应措施增加天然河流流量。

6. Establish regulations to limit human-caused underwater noise levels.

制定法规以限制人为造成的水下噪音水平。
